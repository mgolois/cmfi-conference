About Us

This Prophetic Revival Conference is a unique event not to miss. Christians are coming from all part of the world to attend this life transforming event as we intercede for our country. We strongly ask that all who desire to attend this conference inform us as soon as possible. Our speakers are known worldwide and many who have heard about this program have already made plans to attend. The attendance projected at this time leaves us with limited room capacity. Therefore, the earlier you let us know your intention to attend the easier it will be for us to secure your spaces. Lodging arrangements will be made according to the number of out-of-state participants we will have. We therefore ask those who will need accommodation to please let us know in advance so we can plan accordingly. For all our out-of-state brethren, please let us know when you are landing so we can pick you up at the airport or train stations. Attendance is free.

We entreat you to keep this program in your prayers. Prayer topics will be circulated in due time. The Lord our God will do great things. The glory of God will be manifested at these meetings. We shall all be transformed forever. Our Senior Pastor, Rev Robinson Fondong, believes that we will experience a divine visitation throughout the course of this special program. We implore you not to miss this glorious occasion. We are expecting amazing things to happen in our midst. Our prayer is that permission and visas to attend will be granted to all who need it. You will not regret any sacrifices made to partake in this monumental event. Please feel free to email us at info@cmfimaryland.com, or call Br Paul Kempa (443-508-1770) or Br Golois Mouelet (443-789-6732) with all your inquiries.

Speakers

Prophet Sadhu Sundar

Prophet Sadhu Sundar Selvaraj is a forerunner who has been privileged to be called to prepare the way for the Second Coming of the Lord Jesus Christ. He has been walking in the secret place with God for well over three decades. Brother Sadhu, as he is affectionately called, has had numerous encounters with the Lord and has been commissioned by the Lord Jesus Christ to prepare the Bride for His second coming. For sixteen years, he pioneered an apostolic work to the unreached people of Tibet in Asia where he witnessed major victories over forces of darkness. In 2005, at the command of the Lord, he launched Angel TV, a 24-hour Christian television network, as an end-time prophetic voice to prepare the nations for the Second Coming of the Lord Jesus Christ. Today, Angel TV reaches out to approximately half the globe, well over 125 nations. His life and ministry attested by miracles, signs, and wonders will teach, encourage, and stir you to deepen your walk with Christ. The palpable anointing that flows through Brother Sadhu's life edifies and provokes the saints to live a holy life and to walk intimately with God as the Bride of Christ.
Dr Bruce Allen is the founder of Still Waters International Ministries. He is an author, as well as a well-known international conference speaker and prophetic teacher to the nations. He travels throughout the world ministering the love of Jesus in the character of Christ with unusual signs and wonders. Dr Bruce operates in a remarkable anointing for breakthrough into the glory realm with creative miracles touching the lives of many. A fresh and timely word can be expected as Bruce ministers. It is Dr Bruce’s passion and focus in this hour is to Prepare the Way of the Lord by training, equipping, and activating the Body of Christ for the work of the ministry because this is the hour for the Church to walk in the fullness of their inheritance in Christ. Dr Bruce and his wife, Reshma, live in the Spokane area of Washington, and work together in ministry to awaken a generation to the reality of the Kingdom of God.
