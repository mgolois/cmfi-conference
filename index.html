About Us

This Prophetic Prayer Revival Conference is a unique event not to miss. Christians are coming from all part of the world to attend this life transforming event as we intercede for our country. We strongly ask that all who desire to attend this conference inform us as soon as possible. Dr. Bernard Nwaka is known worldwide and many who have heard about this program have already made plans to attend. The attendance projected at this time leaves us with limited room capacity. Therefore, the earlier you let us know your intention to attend the easier it will be for us to secure your spaces. Lodging arrangements will be made according to the number of out-of-state participants we will have. We therefore ask those who will need accommodation to please let us know in advance so we can plan accordingly. For all our out-of-state brethren, please let us know when you are landing so we can pick you up at the airport or train stations. Attendance is free.

We entreat you to keep this program in your prayers. Prayer topics will be circulated in due time. The Lord our God will do great things. The glory of God will be manifested at these meetings. We shall all be transformed forever. Our Senior Pastor, Rev Robinson Fondong, believes that we will experience a divine visitation throughout the course of this special program. We implore you not to miss this glorious occasion. We are expecting amazing things to happen in our midst. Our prayer is that permission and visas to attend will be granted to all who need it. You will not regret any sacrifices made to partake in this monumental event. Please feel free to email us at info@cmfimaryland.com, or call Br Paul Kempa (443-508-1770) or Br Golois Mouelet (443-789-6732) with all your inquiries.

Speaker

Dr. Bernard Nwaka

Dr. Bernard Nwaka is the founder and presiding Bishop of Living Waters Global Churches and Restoration Bible Churches in Zambia, Cameroon, Tanzania, Namibia the UK, the USA, and South Africa. He operates in the Apostolic and Prophetic ministries and runs Apostolic and Prophetic schools . Bishop Nwaka has been called to uproot demonic alters and to establish alters of prayer and intercession in nations throughout the world. He is called to raise a company of Prophets and Apostles (Raising Sons and Building Ministries) and is the Chancellor of the Living Waters University College. Dr. Bernard is married to wife Joyce Luki and they are blessed with four daughters.
